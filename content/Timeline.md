# World Timeline

- **960** — The first Dark Knight is born. *(story)*
- **1311** — A Werlytian noble is born — Mores Gwydion's lineage. *(blackram)*
- **1462** — [PARTIALLY LEGIBLE — confirm] *(partial)*
- **1472** — Technology advances and harmonizes across the Five Forces. *(xiv)*
- **1498** — First expeditions to Ilsabard. *(xiv)*
- **1520** — **[Blackram] Mores Gwydion leaves his city to stop Nidhogg's advance. Severely wounded, enters stasis for two years. Believed dead — Woeborn is forged from a Nidhogg scale in his honour. He did not forge it himself.** *(blackram)*
- **1521** — **[XIV] Garleans use Magitek to conquer Ilsabard and Othard.
[Blackram] Mores' family is slaughtered. His daughter Mari (age 19) is captured by the Garleans and used in early Oversoul experiments. Mores recovers from stasis to find everything gone.** *(xiv, blackram)*
- **1522** — Mores resolves to find a way to resurrect Mari. Begins searching for a path. *(blackram)*
- **1528** — Mores begins studying Oversoul — the forbidden technique of soul transference. *(blackram)*
- **1537** — **Mores, now 75, has studied Oversoul for 35 years. Performs his first soul transfer to avoid dying before his goal is achieved.** *(blackram)*
- **1552** — Garlemald established as an empire. Cailean Arda born. Lexus Ovicula born. *(xiv, story)*
- **1555** — Namazu rise in prominence. Lexus's sister born. *(story)*
- **1558** — Mores, in a new host, encounters Geciro Teciro (Lociro's brother). *(blackram)*
- **1559** — Mores meets Nunuwa Nuwa — Geciro's wife. Tells her the truth. She becomes an ally. *(blackram)*
- **1561** — Mores creates the Mari mammet using her soul imprint. Calls it a success, though it is not a true resurrection. *(blackram)*
- **1562** — Geciro dies from Blackram disease — a consequence of hosting Mores' soul. The Mari mammet passes to Nunuwa's care. *(blackram)*
- **1563** — Mores' chimera core experiments — attempts to stabilize Mari's soul signature for future resurrection. Results prove insufficient. *(blackram)*
- **1576** — [Blackram] Mores takes Galene Didanc's mother as his next host body.
[Story] Auria Van Varus raids Luvsan's settlement, taking his sister. Luvsan spends years hunting her operations. Mores (in a host body) attempts to Oversoul into Luvsan — rejected. Luvsan becomes a cursed shade. *(blackram, story)*
- **1578** — **[XIV] The Dragonsong War ends.
[Story] Liraz Laskaris, age 23, picks up Luvsan's Dark Knight soul crystal. Through the crystal, Luvsan's shade begins to witness what Auria has been doing to her.** *(xiv, story)*
- **1579** — **Zekito Silverblood arrives in Hydaelyn. His soul dissonance begins. First priority: clothes. Method: theft.** *(story)*
- **1580** — Zekito is implicated in an incident in Ishgard. [Partially confirm] *(story, partial)*
- **1582** — **[XIV] Garlemald is destroyed. The Final Days begin. The Scions of the Seventh Dawn disband.
[Story] Zekito and Nimvaei fall in love. Auria Van Varus is killed. The Lambs of Dalamud are destroyed. Shizu is freed.
[Blackram] Mores Gwydion Oversouls into Orraux Clauvier — his vessel at the start of Runes of Chimera.** *(xiv, story, blackram)*
