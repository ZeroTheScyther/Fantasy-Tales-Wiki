**Luvsan** was a mercenary and Dark Knight operating in Western [[Coerthas]]. He dedicated his life to protecting a small frozen settlement at the edge of the wastes — providing food, gil, and safety to the people who lived there. He had a sister.

He was eventually killed by [[Nimvaei / Liraz Laskaris|Liraz]], who had no idea who he was. He had been trying to free her. His soul crystal, recovered years later without that knowledge, became the connection through which his rage at what [[Auria Van Varus|Auria]] had done to her became her courage — the thing that gave her the strength to finally run.

## History

[[Auria Van Varus]] raided his settlement in one of her operations to collect trafficking victims. His sister was taken. Luvsan spent years hunting Auria's operations in retaliation — slaughtering her men, ruining her income, causing major losses. He was a thorn in her side for a long time.

At some point, [[Mores Gwydion]] — inhabiting a host body — lured him to [[Ishgard]] with a promise of information. The meeting was a trap: Mores attempted to [[Oversoul]] into him. Luvsan's instinct to love and protect was greater than Mores' will. His soul rejected the takeover — but the cost was severe. He became a rage-filled shade, sustained by a single drive: justice.

Two villains destroyed him independently and without ever knowing the other was involved: [[Auria Van Varus|Auria]] took everyone he loved; [[Mores Gwydion|Mores]] tried to take his body.

He was killed during one of Auria's trafficking operations — [[Nimvaei / Liraz Laskaris|Liraz]] was one of the women in the cart being transported to a brothel Auria owned. He was there attacking Auria's men, doing what he always did. From her perspective: a fearsome Dark Knight in the middle of a slaughter. [[Auria Van Varus|Auria's]] look still fresh in her memory, everything in her screaming to run. She had a job to finish first. She put a knife in his exposed neck, pulled it out, and watched him collapse. She didn't know who he was. She didn't care. She was safe. Finally.

In 1578, [[Nimvaei / Liraz Laskaris|Liraz]] picked up his soul crystal — not knowing she had killed the man it belonged to. Through the crystal, he witnessed what [[Auria Van Varus|Auria]] had been doing to her. His rage at that injustice became her courage. He stirred her soul and gave her the strength to run.

## Plots

### Runes of Chimera — The Woeborn Fight

Inside [[Woeborn]], Luvsan's shade manifests as [[Nimvaei / Liraz Laskaris|Liraz's]] fear of her own Dark Knight abilities. The [[Woeborn]] fight forces her to relive the full memory — the cart, the other women, him attacking Auria's men, the knife, his collapse — and understand, for the first time, what she had actually been seeing.

She can only defeat the shade by **letting it stab her** — by stopping running from what she is. When she does, the shadow becomes her blade. Luvsan goes dormant. He passed the torch. He was never an antagonist. He was the person who died trying to free her, whose death she caused without knowing it, whose anger at that injustice became the thing that saved her.
