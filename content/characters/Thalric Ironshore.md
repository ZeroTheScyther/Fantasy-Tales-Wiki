**Thalric Ironshore** — formerly Thalric Ironmace — is a salvager and handyman at [[The Silver Bazaar]]. The name change came with the retirement from fighting. He scours the shores and ruins around the Bazaar, retrieving and repairing items to sell. Specializes in fixing broken goods — pots, nets, lanterns — and handles odd jobs around the Bazaar when not salvaging.

Pragmatic and stoic. Carries an undercurrent of melancholy from the loss of his old life — rarely speaks of his past. Fiercely loyal to the people of the Bazaar; sees them as his family. While he avoids conflict, he won't hesitate to step in if someone is being mistreated. His presence alone is enough to deter most troublemakers.
