**Thalric Ironshore** — formerly Thalric Ironmace — is a salvager and handyman at [[The Silver Bazaar]].

## Appearance

Broad-shouldered and powerfully built, though slower now from age and an old injury. Dark brown hair, short and untidy, shot through with gray, and a thick silver-streaked beard. Deep-set gray eyes. A jagged scar runs from his right shoulder across his chest — the wound that ended his fighting days. He wears a patched leather jerkin, heavy muddy boots, and keeps salvaging tools on his belt.

The name change came with the retirement from fighting. He scours the shores and ruins around the Bazaar, retrieving and repairing items to sell. Specializes in fixing broken goods — pots, nets, lanterns — and handles odd jobs around the Bazaar when not salvaging.

Pragmatic and stoic. Carries an undercurrent of melancholy from the loss of his old life — rarely speaks of his past. Fiercely loyal to the people of the Bazaar; sees them as his family. While he avoids conflict, he won't hesitate to step in if someone is being mistreated. His presence alone is enough to deter most troublemakers.
