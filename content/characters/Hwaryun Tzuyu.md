**Hwaryun Tzuyu** (known as Ryun) is the markswoman of the Rose and Her Thorns. Extremely confident in her marksmanship — her go-to weapon since before Lunar Rose. The hesitance comes when she has to make a decision, not when she has to pull a trigger.

## Personality

| Trait | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| Strengths | Expert markswoman — her go-to weapon since before Lunar Rose. Very confident in marksmanship. |
| Weakness | Has never taken a life (leads to hesitance in combat). Impulsive when [[Kiku Kha\|Kiku]] is threatened. Too honest and open. Indecisive under forced split-second choices — acts once someone she trusts moves first. |
| Hates | Anyone hurting or threatening her friends. Anyone forcing major split-moment decisions. |
