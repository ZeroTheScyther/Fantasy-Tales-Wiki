**Nunuwa Nuwa** is the wife of Geciro Teciro and an ally of [[Mores Gwydion]]. When Mores encountered Geciro in 1558 and met Nunuwa the following year, he told her the truth about who he was and what he was trying to do. She believed him — and helped.

## Appearance

Nunuwa is a Lalafell with fair skin and blonde hair worn in twin tails. She favours simple, soft-coloured clothing — a pale pink dress. She has large, dark eyes and a childlike build typical of her race, but her manner is measured and deliberate.
