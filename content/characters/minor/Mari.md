**Mari** is the daughter of [[Mores Gwydion]] and the reason he spent over a century body-hopping across Hydaelyn. She died at nineteen when Garlean forces used her in early Oversoul experiments in 1521. Her soul was imprinted and eventually housed within [[Woeborn]]. A mammet built from her soul imprint served as the closest thing to her that Mores could create — though it was never a true resurrection.

## Appearance

The Mari mammet appears as a young woman with dark red hair worn in a loose bun. She dresses simply — a yellow-green jacket over a school-style outfit with a black bow tie, practical ankle boots. She looks exactly as she did at nineteen. The face Mores spent sixty years trying to bring back.
