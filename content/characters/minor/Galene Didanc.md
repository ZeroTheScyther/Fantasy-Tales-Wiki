**Galene Didanc** is a Miqo'te with a notable connection to [[Mores Gwydion]]'s history: in 1576, Mores took her mother's body as his next host vessel.

## Appearance

Galene is a Miqo'te woman with auburn hair and cat ears. She has a sharp, composed look — dark lips, dark clothing. Her expression in the single recorded image suggests someone accustomed to keeping their own counsel.
