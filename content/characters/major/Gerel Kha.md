**Gerel Kha** is a mage based in Ul'dah. Pragmatic and honest, she was the first person in [[Hydaelyn]] to correctly identify what was wrong with [[Zekito Silverblood|Zekito]]. She diagnosed the aetherial disturbance caused by his soul dissonance when his condition was worsening, and gave him the harder truth: he had been fighting like he was still in [[Erinn]]. He was not anymore.

## Role

Gerel directed [[Zekito Silverblood|Zekito]] to two sources of help: a magitek engineer who built him stabilizing armour to manage the dissonance physically, and the [[Lunar Rose Adventuring Academy]], where he could find longer-term treatment and support. Without her intervention, his deterioration would have continued unchecked.
