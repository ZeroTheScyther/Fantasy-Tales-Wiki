**Lunair Blackwind** is a teacher at the [[Lunar Rose Adventuring Academy]] and, by his own admission, an aetherial mad scientist. He is the person who stabilized [[Zekito Silverblood|Zekito's]] soul dissonance — first with a temporary arcana seal placed at Zekito's request to buy time, and later with a far more invasive procedure that effectively rebuilt him from the inside out.

## Role

### The Arcana Seal

During [[Zekito Silverblood|Zekito's]] time at Lunar Rose, Lunair placed an arcana seal on him at his request — a temporary measure to stabilize the soul dissonance while a more permanent solution was developed. The seal manifested as visible markings. It was stripped away by [[Auria Van Varus|Auria's]] men during the [[Fury's Gaze]] arc, which accelerated Zekito's deterioration into psychosis during his captivity.

### The Rebuild

After the [[Fury's Gaze]] arc resolved, Lunair used repurposed voidsent machinery — originally [[Auria Van Varus|Auria's]] — to break [[Zekito Silverblood|Zekito]] apart at an aetherial level and rebuild a body compatible with his soul's actual signature. A second fragment of Zekito's soul was then transferred from the [[Auracite Bracelet|bracelet]] into the new body, raising him from 1/10 to 2/10 of his original demigod soul. The procedure caused partial amnesia.
