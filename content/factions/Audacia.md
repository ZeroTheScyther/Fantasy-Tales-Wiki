**Audacia** is an adventurer's guild in [[Erinn]]. The name means boldness. Led by **Eriline**, whose iron-fisted leadership drove members to leave and form rival guild [[Nixia]]. [[Zekito Silverblood|Zekito]] was a respected member; many sought his counsel.
