The **Lambs of Dalamud** are a fractured religious cult operating across multiple regions of [[Hydaelyn]]. They summon voidsent by sacrificing captives or themselves. Multiple semi-independent sects with infrequent communication operate across Northern Thanalan, South Shroud (Amdapor Ruins), and North Shroud.

They fell on hard times after the Final Days when captives they held began transforming into monsters and turned on their own ranks. Their operations continued regardless — they are currently running **Operation Dalamud** in Western Coerthas, a revenge operation for the Ahriman incident [[Zekito Silverblood|Zekito]] had been tracking.

## Zekito's Investigation

[[Zekito Silverblood|Zekito]] has been tracking the Lambs as part of a broader investigation into disappearances of young women (ages 16–22, primarily Miqo'te, Hyur, and Au Ra, from poor/refugee backgrounds) around Ul'dah. Working theory: the Lambs are deliberately targeting transformation-prone individuals exposed to Akasa, believing their sensitivity enhances voidsent summoning rituals.

During the Ishgard Journey arc, a Lamb group ambushed [[Zekito Silverblood|Zekito]] and [[Nimvaei / Liraz Laskaris|Nimvaei]] between Fallgourd Float and Florentel's Spire. The group's leader sacrificed herself to summon the [[Blackguard]], a humanoid winged voidsent capable of speech and aether drain. [[Zekito Silverblood|Zekito]] was impaled; [[Nimvaei / Liraz Laskaris|Nimvaei]] killed the Blackguard but retains no memory of the fight.
