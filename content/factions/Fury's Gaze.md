**Fury's Gaze** is a private security company that operated as a front for [[Umbra]]'s interests in Eorzea. Publicly, it presented itself as a mercenary and security outfit — managing bodyguard contracts, enforcement work, and logistics. In practice, it was [[Umbra]]'s ground-level operations arm: a vehicle for mercenary recruitment, sabotage, surveillance, and cover for [[Auria Van Varus|Auria's]] research program. The company's physical anchor was the research facility in the Western Coerthas Highlands, now known as [[Auria's Lab]].

## Leadership

[[Lexus Ovicula]] — an [[Umbra]] Phantom — ran Fury's Gaze as its visible face. He managed cells, organized mercenary groups, and ran surface operations while [[Auria Van Varus|Auria]] pursued her research behind the front. Fury's Gaze gave Umbra's field operations a legal surface to hide behind.

## Operations

Fury's Gaze conducted open mercenary work as legitimate cover while coordinating Umbra's active missions beneath it. Known activities include:

- Operating cells across Eorzea under the surface company name
- Delivering Garlean schematics to Vangarde, setting up the Grimstone mining deal at Burning Walls
- Running sabotage and surveillance operations in support of [[Umbra]] objectives
- Managing [[Auria Van Varus|Auria's]] subject acquisition and transport to [[Auria's Lab]]

## Notes

Fury's Gaze as an organization effectively ceased to exist after [[Auria Van Varus|Auria's]] death in 1582. [[Lexus Ovicula]] continued operating under [[Umbra]] directly. The research facility retained the Fury's Gaze name colloquially, though it has since been referred to as [[Auria's Lab]].
