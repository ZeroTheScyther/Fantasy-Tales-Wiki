**Nixia** is a splinter guild formed by ex-[[Audacia]] members unhappy with Eriline's leadership. Led by **Vavi**. [[Mewecho]] — [[Zekito Silverblood|Zekito's]] twin sister, though neither knew it at the time — served as an officer here during the guild conflict.
