**Lunar Rose Adventuring Academy** (commonly referred to as **Lunar Rose** or **Lunar Rose Academy**) is an adventuring academy in [[Hydaelyn]] that trains aspiring adventurers and provides them with a structured path from raw recruits to field-capable operatives. Its training regimen is rigorous; its dropout rate is not trivial.

[[Zekito Silverblood|Zekito]] enrolled in 1581, directed there by mage Gerel Kha following the diagnosis of his soul dissonance. [[Nimvaei / Liraz Laskaris|Nimvaei]] was already present. What began as a student placement became, for both of them, the beginning of everything that followed.

## Notes

The informal name given to Nimvaei's party — **The Rose and Her Thorns** — was coined by [[Amelia Graves]] as a barb during the [[Fury's Gaze]] arc. The Rose referring to Nimvaei; the Thorns to those around her. The party's actual institutional affiliation is Lunar Rose Adventuring Academy.
