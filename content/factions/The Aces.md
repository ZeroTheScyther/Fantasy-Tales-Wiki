**The Aces** are an elite group of the most powerful adventurers in [[Erinn]]. [[Merlin]] is a member. [[Zekito Silverblood|Zekito]] spent four years travelling with them under [[Merlin]]'s mentorship, receiving Snap Cast, Spellwalk, and his custom knuckles. [[Merlin]] invented all of the weapons used by the group.
