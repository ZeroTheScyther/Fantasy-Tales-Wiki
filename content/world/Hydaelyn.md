> # Hydaelyn


**Hydaelyn** is the second world of Fantasy Tales, technologically and magically advanced compared to [[Erinn]]. When [[Zekito Silverblood|Zekito]] arrives, he is stripped of his [[Fomorian Blood|Fomorian power]], reduced to an ordinary sickly Miqo'te body, and subject to soul dissonance — his demigod soul slowly destroying the body that cannot contain it.
