> # Erinn


**Erinn** is the source world of Fantasy Tales — where [[Zekito Silverblood]] was born and spent the majority of his early life. A world of magic, myth, and ancient power. Milletians — beings from other worlds — exist in Erinn as a known phenomenon, treated with curiosity and wariness.

## Regions & Key Locations

| Location | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Uladh | The western region — more civilized. Contains Tir Chonaill, Dunbarton, and Tara. |
| [[Tir Chonaill]] | A small farming village in Uladh. Zekito's adoptive home and the place that banished him. |
| [[Dunbarton]] | A larger town in Uladh. Zekito spent a year here as a street rat after banishment. |
| [[Dugald Forest]] | Forest connecting Tir Chonaill and Dunbarton. Site of the encounter with the adventurer who gave Zekito his first knuckles. |
| Tara | Capital city of Uladh. Hub of commerce and politics. |

| Vales | Northern cold lands. Where [[Mewecho]] was forced to grow up alone. |
| The Silver Kingdom | Isanshiro's kingdom, built for the destitute. Destroyed by the Knights of Light. |

## Nature of Erinn

Erinn accommodates both humans and [[Fomorian Blood|Fomorians]] — ancient, powerful beings of primal magic. Half-breeds (those born of both) are rare and carry immense power, but that power is volatile. [[Fomorian Blood|Fomorian blood]] expresses as berserk states where the host loses all control. Bracelets enchanted by powerful mages can suppress this, but the blood is never truly gone.

## Magic System

Magic in Erinn typically requires a **focus** — a staff or wand — to channel. Higher-level magic without a focus is considered extremely difficult and rare. [[Zekito Silverblood|Zekito]] is one of the very few who can do this, thanks to his custom knuckles crafted by [[Merlin]] and the techniques Merlin taught him (Snap Cast, Spellwalk).
