**The Auracite Bracelet** is a mythril bracelet originating from [[Erinn]], where such items are called mythril. In [[Hydaelyn]], the material is classified as Auracite. There are two of them — one worn by [[Zekito Silverblood|Zekito]], one by [[Mewecho Silverblood|Mewecho]] — and they are attuned to each other. They were originally crafted as suppressants for the twins' [[Fomorian Blood]], then enhanced by [[Merlin]] to expand their function.

## Function

The bracelets serve several purposes simultaneously:

**Suppression.** The bracelet passively amasses ambient aether to suppress [[Fomorian Blood|Fomorian hunger]] — the instinct that, left unchecked, drives half-Fomorians toward consumption and eventual feral collapse. Without it, [[Zekito Silverblood|Zekito]] cannot safely function in aether-rich environments for extended periods.

**Soul containment.** Zekito's bracelet currently contains 8/10 of his original demigod soul. When he arrived in [[Hydaelyn]], only 1/10 of his soul had transferred into his body — the remaining 9/10 sat in the bracelet. After [[Lunair Blackwind]]'s rebuild procedure, a second fragment was infused from the bracelet into the new body, leaving only 8/10 of Zekito's original soul in the bracelet.

**Sibling attunement.** The two bracelets are attuned to each other. [[Zekito Silverblood|Zekito]] can always locate [[Mewecho Silverblood|Mewecho]] through his, and vice versa. No distance — including the gap between worlds — has severed this link.

**Portal anchor.** Zekito's plan to return to [[Erinn]]: breach the Crystal Tower, use it as an aetherial battery to amplify the bracelet's attunement until it tears open a portal to [[Mewecho Silverblood|Mewecho's]] bracelet. It has not been attempted yet.
