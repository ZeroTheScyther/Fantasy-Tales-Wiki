**Woeborn** is a sword forged from one of Nidhogg's scales. It was made in [[Mores Gwydion|Mores Gwydion's]] honour while he was presumed dead following the battle with Nidhogg in 1520. When he recovered from stasis to find his family slaughtered and his daughter [[Mari]] taken, Woeborn became the vessel he used to preserve what remained of her soul.

## Contents

- **Nidhogg's rage** — the baseline. It tries to consume souls confined within.
- **[[Luvsan Tumet]]** — dormant, after passing the torch to [[Nimvaei / Liraz Laskaris|Liraz]].
- **[[Mores Gwydion|Mores]]** — dormant, eventually at peace. He is with [[Mari]] again, in a sense.
- **Nimvaei** — [[Nimvaei / Liraz Laskaris|Liraz's]] living shadow.

The sword is soul-bound to [[Nimvaei / Liraz Laskaris|Liraz]]. Half of her soul resides inside the sword. All inhabitants can communicate with her telepathically.

## Connection to the Trance

[[Nimvaei / Liraz Laskaris|Nimvaei's]] first involuntary [[Nimvaei / Liraz Laskaris#trance|Trance]] was triggered when someone stabbed her with Woeborn, forcing the draconic fusion on her. Every subsequent activation requires self-stabbing and winning a battle of wills against Nidhogg's rage.
